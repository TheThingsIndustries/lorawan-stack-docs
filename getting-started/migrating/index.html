Migrating to The Things Stack

This guide documents the process of migrating end devices and gateways to The Things Stack.

End devices can be migrated from one The Things Stack deployment to another (e.g. from The Things Stack Community Edition to The Things Stack Cloud) or from any other LoRaWAN network (like Chirpstack).

Depending on how many devices you are migrating and which network you are migrating from, there are multiple migration methods you can apply. Find your specific use case in subsections below and learn how to migrate your devices to a desired The Things Stack deployment.
